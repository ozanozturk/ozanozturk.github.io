PAW & ORDER

Paw & Order is a story-driven adventure game, developed both as a point and click and HTML-based text adventure game.

A long time ago in a forest not so far away, there was a war. It was a battle between dogs, cats and birds; a calamitous conflict between three species of animals that once lived in peace and harmony.

The battle resulted in a crowning victory for the feline faction. The dogs were left disenfranchised and the birds remained mostly unaffected, being avian creatures with the ability to reach beyond the furry claw of the Cat's domain.

A reign of uneasy peace ruled the forest, marred by outbreaks of unkind words and political incorrectness just below the surface of the society. The survivors of this great tragedy suffer from old wounds.

Paw & Order is the story of a hound who embarks on a voyage which will take him far into the treacherous tides of tooth, talon and claw.
