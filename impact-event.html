IMPACT EVENT

Impact Event is a 2D space shooter prototype developed with Unity 4.6.

The goal of the game is to destroy the approaching asteroid before it hits the planet. 

The game requires players to be careful and multitask, as not only the asteroid is covered with a revolving layer of meteoroids that serve as a shield, but there's also an incoming meteor storm that can cause significant damage to the planet.

Moreover, the player ship can be moved only around the circular orbit of the planet they are defending, forcing the player to carefully position their ship to hit the targets.

Fortunately, occasional power-ups drop down the screen to assist the sole defender of the planet.
