GAMEPLAY DESIGN


HEART_DRIVE

PC | Single Player | Narrative Typing Game

HEART_DRIVE is a story-driven typing game that puts you in the shoes of an artificial intelligence trying to prove itself "human" on a first date.

In this project, I bridged gameplay and narrative in a tight game loop and delivered a branching narrative with high replayability.

Gameplay Design, Narrative Design, UI Design

L.A.M.E.

PC, Mobile | Multiplayer | First-Person Shooter, Real-Time Strategy

L.A.M.E. is an experimental mix of first-person shooter (PC) and real-time strategy (mobile) gameplay in local multiplayer.

In this project, I designed a high-mobility shooter gameplay featuring a reactive progression system for two players in asymmetric roles.

SCHACHT

PC & PS4 | Single Player & Multiplayer | 3D Platformer

Run, dash and jump! Schacht is a speed-driven 3D platformer feauting unlimited replayability thanks to billions of procedurally-generated levels.

In this project, I iterated on the design of the game in accordance with player feedback and contributed to procedural generation of game levels.
