Hello, and welcome to my portfolio!

My name is Ozan. I am a game designer and producer with over four years of game dev experience.

I hold a Master's degree in Game Technologies and I am currently specializing further in Game Design at the Cologne Game Lab where I also participate in academic research projects.

Developing games is my greatest passion and having experienced the creative process with over 100 individuals from all over the world is my privilege.

You can scroll down below to learn more about some of the collaborative projects I have worked on. If you would like to get in touch, please feel free to drop me a line via e-mail, or on Twitter or LinkedIn.
