MERCEDES-BENZ
POCKET MUSEUM


GENRE: Collection          PLATFORM: iOS, Android          ENGINE: Unity

ROLE: Game Designer          TEAM: +25          DURATION: +9 months
Mercedes-Benz Pocket Museum invites visitors to playfully explore the exhibitions at the Mercedes-Benz Museum. For this purpose, the visitor, led by a famous person of history such as Karl or Bertha Benz, can create their own collection of exhibits.
The purpose of the game is not only to entertain the young visitors of the museum, but also draw their attention to the finer details of the history and craftsmanship of Mercedes-Benz through simple-yet-entertaining minigames.
​
Using the app in the museum, visitors can engage with historical figures such as Karl Benz, Wilhelm Maybach and Gottlieb Daimler. These characters will guide them in exhibitions and assists them in collecting unique objects--namely cars. The final version game will feature over 20 collectible cars with various unlockable customization options.
The game is still in development and will debut in the Mercedes-Benz Museum in 2018. Please note that features and visuals may be subject to change.
