DRAGONSLAYERS


Jump into the fray with your friends and slay a fearsome dragon!

* Create your hero, position yourself and roll the die to make your move.

* Use magic items to empower your companions.

* Strategize together to bring the apex predator down.

PreviousNextClose

GENRE: Strategy Card Game

PLAYERS: 4

Description

Dragonslayers is a turn-based strategy card game for a group of four brave heroes who must face a fierce dragon in combat.

Before the battle starts, you must pick a character trait and synergize your strengths and weaknesses with your choice of weapon.

Positioning is key in Dragonslayers. Where you sit on the table determines which parts of the colossal dragon you are in range of. If you're carrying a melee weapon, you can critically hit the dragon's legs and tail, whereas ranged attacks are more effective against the dragon's head and wings.

Magic in the world of Dragonslayers is achieved through the transference of one’s spiritual energies through enchanted items. A limited number of such items are available on the table and can be used to weaken the dragon momentarily or support your companions in the fight against the apex predator. You must be mindful of how you use these cards, however, as they are consumed on use.

The dragon will retaliate every turn and unleash terrifying attacks against your party determined by the die roll. You must consider every action carefully for you risk angering the beast further with each strike, which could lead to the dragon performing deadly special moves.

Dragonslayers is currently in playtesting stage and will hit Kickstarter soon with a brand new art direction. 

Key Features

CREATE YOUR HERO: Build your own hero with a character trait and unique weapon. Synergize your strengths and weaknesses with your attacks. Will you fight with a brutish warhammer or a nimble dagger? The choice is yours.

MAKE YOUR MOVE: Where will you attack the dragon? How will you use your weapon? Evaluate your actions carefully and coordinate with your companions before you roll the die. 

DEFEND YOUR FRIENDS: A dragon's wrath is one to behold. Your only chances of survival lies in collaboration. When the situation is dire, use magic items to bolster the strength of your fellow heroes or inflict a curse upon the vicious beast.
