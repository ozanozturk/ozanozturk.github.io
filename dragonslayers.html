DRAGONSLAYERS


GENRE: Strategy Card Game

PLAYERS: 4

PreviousNextClose

Jump into the fray with your friends and slay a fearsome dragon!

• Create your hero, position yourself and roll the die to make your move.
• Use magic items to empower your companions.
• Strategize together to bring the apex predator down.

Features

CREATE YOUR HERO: Pick a character trait and synergize your strengths and weaknesses with a unique weapon. Will you fight with a brutish warhammer or a nimble dagger? The choice is yours.

MAKE YOUR MOVE: Where will you attack the dragon? How will you use your weapon? Coordinate with your companions before you roll the die. Evaluate your actions carefully or risk the wrath of a dragon.

DEFEND YOUR COMPANIONS: A dragon's wrath is one to behold. Your only chances of survival lies in collaboration. When the situation is dire, use magic items to bolster the strength of your fellow heroes or inflict a curse upon the vicious beast.

Context

Dragonslayers is an example of how I can carry game concepts from digital to non-digital means to prototype and deliver a compelling gameplay experience across mediums.

This project spun off from a rapid prototyping assignment I received at the Cologne Game Lab. My intention was to adapt the 4v1 dragon battles of Dragon Age Inquisition into a cooperative card game. After the great success of the initial playtesting sessions, I decided to pursue it further, testing and tweaking it one variable at a time.

Dragonslayers will soon hit Kickstarter with a brand new art direction.
