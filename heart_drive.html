HEART_DRIVE


Take on the role of an android on a date with an unassuming human stranger!

Take part in a field test to prove your ability to blend into human society.

Type and combine available words into sentences however you like.

Convince your date of your humanity and win their heart.

HEART_DRIVE is a narrative game project developed by 5 students of Cologne Game Lab in a short span of 6 weeks.

In this project, I took on all production and design-related duties in this project, including those of project management, gameplay and UI design and writing.

GENRE: Typing

PLATFORM: PC

ENGINE: Unity

TEAM: 5

ROLE: Producer & Designer

DURATION: 6 weeks
