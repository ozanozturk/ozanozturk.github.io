SUPER COMBAT HIGH

In Super Combat High, you play as a student trying to graduate from high school by achieving success in levels themed after high school subjects, working with or against other students and overcoming the deadly challenges prepared by their teachers.

Gameplay involves traversing hand-built environments and exploring the domain of the teachers, doing some platforming and engaging in melee combat with other students.

Every level is a world of its own, related in some way or other to the high school subject they are about.


Super Combat High is currently in development.

​COMMENTS

The concept of Super Combat High was pitched by me. The game is currently in pre-production and the team working on it consists of myself as the only game designer, a project manager, four programmers and a sound designer.

Unfortunately, we have not had dedicated artists to work with on this project so far, and this left me with the task of creating and finding royalty-free art assets to be used in the level. Realizing the humor of this unique game concept with a very limited number of original assets proved to be the greatest challenge I faced during the development process of Super Combat High.

The level I built was a Chemistry level, a subject taught by a literal witch who lives in a castle and brews potions in her dungeons. To explain this concept to the players without any text or cutscenes:
  • A sequence of the flying witch and her castle was made visible on the school hallway where the players pick a subject and access the levels,
  • A picture of a pointy hat was shown above the name of the teacher on the level select screen,
  • Various level sequences of the witch flying in the background and dropping various potion bottles were placed in both the classroom and dungeon parts of the level.

The resulting level received satisfactory feedback from the players who played the game at Media Night of Stuttgart Media University, as they were able to understand the concept simply by playing the prototype from start to finish without any explanations.
