SUPER COMBAT HIGH


Super Combat High is a co-op beat 'em up that puts you in the shoes of a high school student facing the deadly challenges set up by his teacher.

GENRE: Beat 'Em Up 
PLATFORM: PC 
ENGINE: Unreal Engine 4 
TEAM: 7 
ROLE: Project Lead // Game Designer // Level Designer
DURATION: 6 months, finished development

HIGH SCHOOL ACTION

Play alone or with a friend in levels themed after school subjects. Beat rival classmates. Punch your way to a passing grade.

CHEMISTRY 101

Explore the chemistry teacher Ms. Garlick's dungeon. Avoid the deadly concoctions she brew against teenage vermin.

GRADES MATTER

Grab lecture notes from your fallen colleagues to boost your final grade.

Super Combat High demonstrates how I can leverage scope constraints and mold them into a game's design.

My responsibilities in this project covered all aspects of game and level design as well as project management. Some of my tasks were:

• Creating and updating the game design document,
• Designing a melee combat system featuring movesets and frame data for 3 character classes (only one of which was developed),
• Specifying the AI behavior to govern 4 different enemy types, 
• Creating the Chemistry 101 level in Unreal Engine 4, including cinematic and scripted events, using self-made and free assets to convey the humorous narrative of the game, 
• Monitoring the project, prioritizing and distributing tasks on JIRA. 

My accomplishment of note in this project was creating the game's level without a level artist. The challenge of realizing the humor of the unique game concept without unique assets was a constraint I used to the advantage of the game's design in the end: The concept of a witch teacher and her dungeons as the game's level was devised around the fantasy environment assets released for free by Epic Games.
