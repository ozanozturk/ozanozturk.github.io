SUPER COMBAT HIGH

In Super Combat High, you play as a student trying to graduate from high school by achieving success in levels themed after high school subjects, working with or against other students and overcoming the deadly challenges prepared by their teachers.

Gameplay involves traversing hand-built environments and exploring the domain of the teachers, doing some platforming and engaging in melee combat with other students.

Every level is a world of its own, related in some way or other to the high school subject they are about.


Super Combat High is currently in development.
