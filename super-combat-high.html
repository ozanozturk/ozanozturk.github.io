SUPER COMBAT HIGH


Don a uniform and punch your way through high school!

* Choose your character and participate in levels themed after school subjects.

* Team up with fellow students to overcome the deadly challenges prepared by the teachers.

* Face the teachers themselves in boss battles to pass or fail the course.

Super Combat High is a beat 'em up prototype developed by a team of students at Stuttgart Media University. 

Having no dedicated artists on this project left me with the task of creating and finding royalty-free art assets to be used in the level. Realizing the humor of this unique game concept with a very limited number of original assets was the greatest obstacle I overcame during the development of this upbeat game.

My responsibilities in this project covered every aspect of game and level design as well as project management.

GENRE: Beat 'em up

PLATFORM: PC

ENGINE: Unreal Engine 4

TEAM: 7

ROLE: Game & Level Designer

DURATION: 6 months

▼ GAME DESIGN TASKS

- Created and updated the game design wiki on Confluence.

- Designed a melee combat system, featuring movesets for 3 player character classes in addition to 4 unique weapon abilities, complete with frame data and balancing details.

- Detailed the AI behavior to govern 4 different enemy types.

- Designed the basics of loot and stats system to later introduce character progression mechanics to the game. 

▼ LEVEL DESIGN TASKS

- Designed and built the Chemistry 101 level in its entirety.

- Created numerous cinematic level sequences to enhance the level concept.
