Hello, and welcome to my portfolio!

My name is Ozan. I am a game designer with over four years of experience.

I hold a Master's degree in Game Technologies and I am currently specializing further in Game Design at the Cologne Game Lab where I also participate in research projects.

Developing games is my passion, and having experienced the creative process with over 100 individuals from all over the world is my privilege.

I have so far explored many different facets of game development and learned to work well in conjunction with other disciplines through firsthand experience.

As a game designer, my interests for further specialization include—but are not limited to—systems design, the reconcilition of gameplay systems with interactive narratives, and player engagement through in-game rewards and progression.

You can scroll down below to learn more about some of the collaborative projects I have worked on. If you would like to get in touch, please feel free to drop me a line via e-mail, or on Twitter or LinkedIn.
