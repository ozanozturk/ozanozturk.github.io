HELLO THERE!

I am a game designer with multifaceted experience in systems, level, narrative and interface design across different genres of gaming. I have worked on games from pitch to release and enjoyed the creative process in diverse teams of talented individuals from different backgrounds and disciplines.

I am interested in designing engrossing games players will not want to let go of. I have so far experimented with systems such as procedural content generation, in-game progression and branching narratives to engage players.

Please scroll down to check out some of the games I have worked on. If you would like to get in touch, go ahead and drop me a line!  

- Ozan

L.A.M.E.

PC & ANDROID | MULTIPLAYER | FPS & RTS

L.A.M.E. is an experimental mix of first-person shooter (PC) and real-time strategy (mobile) gameplay in local multiplayer.

In this project, I designed high-mobility shooter gameplay featuring a reactive progression system for two players in asymmetric roles.

HEART_DRIVE

PC | SINGLE PLAYER | NARRATIVE TYPING GAME

HEART_DRIVE is a story-driven typing game that puts you in the shoes of an artificial intelligence trying to prove itself "human" on a first date.

In this project, I bridged gameplay with narrative in a tight game loop and delivered a branching narrative with high replayability.

SCHACHT

PC & PS4 | SINGLE & MULTIPLAYER | 3D PLATFORMER
Run, dash, jump! Schacht is an infinitely replayable 3D speedrunner featuring billions of procedurally generated levels and challenges.

In this project, I iterated on the game in accordance with player feedback and contributed to procedural generation of game content.

APPETITE

MOBILE | SINGLE PLAYER | STRATEGY CARD GAME

Appetite is a strategy card game where you must serve your insatiable customer with food while ensuring he's "fit" enough to keep paying.

In this project, I designed an accessible experience for mobile platforms featuring an in-game economy balanced around real life energy values.
