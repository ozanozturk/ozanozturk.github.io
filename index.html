Hello there!

I am a game designer with experience in systems, level, narrative and interface design across different genres of gaming. I have worked on games from pitch to release and experienced the creative process in diverse teams of talented individuals from different backgrounds and disciplines.

I have experimented with systems such as procedural content generation, in-game progression and branching narratives to create engaging games players will want to experience more than once.

If you would like to work together, feel free to drop me a line or connect with me on Twitter or LinkedIn.

HEART_DRIVE

PC | Single Player | Narrative Typing Game

HEART_DRIVE is a story-driven typing game that puts you in the shoes of an artificial intelligence trying to prove itself "human" on a first date.

In this project, I bridged gameplay with narrative in a tight game loop and delivered a branching narrative with high replayability.

L.A.M.E.

PC, Mobile | Multiplayer | First-Person Shooter, Real-Time Strategy

L.A.M.E. is an experimental mix of first-person shooter (PC) and real-time strategy (mobile) gameplay in local multiplayer.

In this project, I designed high-mobility shooter gameplay featuring a reactive progression system for two players in asymmetric roles.

SCHACHT

PC & PS4 | Single Player & Multiplayer | 3D Platformer

Run, dash and jump! Schacht is a speed-driven 3D platformer feauting unlimited replayability thanks to billions of procedurally-generated levels.

APPETITE

Mobile | Single Player | Digital Card Game

Appetite is a strategy card game where you must serve your insatiable customer with as much food as possible while ensuring he's "fit" enough to keep paying.

In this project, I designed an accessible experience for mobile platforms featuring an in-game economy balanced around real life energy values of food and drinks.
