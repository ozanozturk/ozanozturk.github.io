M²

M² is an ecosystem that enables mass scale social and gameplay experiences for up to 20,000 players in interlinked worlds with a shared digital asset economy.

As the design lead of M² consumer experience, I am the driver of all user-facing platform features used by projects of famous brands. My key responsibilities include co-authoring product vision, driving design direction, defining feature set, leading multidisciplinary teams and setting quality criteria.

CRYSIS 4

Crysis 4 is the long-anticipated installment in the revolutionary first-person shooter franchise that is famous for pushing the boundaries of what is possible.

As the first and only game designer during concept exploration phase of this ambitious project, I was responsible for overseeing the design and implementation of a wide variety of gameplay systems and features such as 3Cs, game loop, abilities, weapons, enemy AI, progression and more.
