HELLO THERE!

I am a game designer with AAA experience and versatile skillset in gameplay, level, narrative and UX design across different genres of gaming.

I am currently working on Far Cry 6 at Ubisoft Berlin.

I aim to create engaging experiences players will want to experience for a long time and more than once. I am deeply interested in formal game design practices and the psychology of player motivation on which I have written my thesis while working at Ubisoft in support of my design work.

Please scroll down to check out some games I have worked on. If you would like to get in touch, don't hesitate to drop me a line!  

- Ozan

L.A.M.E.

PC & MOBILE | FPS & RTS HYBRID

L.A.M.E. is an experimental mix of first-person shooter (PC) and real-time strategy (mobile) gameplay in local PvP multiplayer.

In this project, I designed a high-mobility shooter with a reactive progression system for two players in asymmetric roles.

HEART_DRIVE

PC | NARRATIVE TYPING GAME

HEART_DRIVE is a story-driven typing game that puts you in the shoes of an artificial intelligence trying to prove itself "human" on a first date.

In this project, I bridged gameplay with narrative in a tight game loop and delivered a branching story with high replayability.

THE CONJURERS

MOBILE | RPG
The Conjurers is a turn-based local multiplayer RPG where communication is as integral to your success as the equipment you bring to battle.

In this project, I designed a communication-driven multiplayer experience and validated my designs via rapid analog playtests every step of the way.

APPETITE

MOBILE | STRATEGY CARD GAME

Appetite is a strategy card game where you must serve your insatiable customer with food while ensuring he's "fit" enough to keep paying.
