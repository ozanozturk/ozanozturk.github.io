HELLO THERE!

I am a driven game designer with experience in a diverse range of projects from mobile to AAA. I specialize in gameplay and engagement design and have worked with game modes, UX, level and narrative design.

Leveraging a versatile design skillset, keen understanding of player motivation and strong passion for the craft, I aim to push the boundaries of engagement in ambitious and innovative titles.

Please scroll down to check out some of the games I have worked on and don't hesitate to drop me a line if you'd like to get in touch!
