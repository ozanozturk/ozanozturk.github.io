TWIN SPROUTS

ITCH.IO

GENRE: 3D Platformer

PLATFORM: PC, Mac

ENGINE: Unity

TEAM: 4

ROLE: Project Manager // Game Designer

DURATION: 10 weeks, shipped

PreviousNextClose

Pick up seeds and grow a vibrant garden!

• Feed your plants with water and sun spells to help them grow.
• Play cooperatively with or competitively against a friend.
• Save your individual and combined team high scores on the leaderboard.

Features

MAJESTIC PLANTS: Grow your garden from seeds to fully-grown plants. Beware the needs of your garden and do not plant more than you can take care of!

MAGIC SPELLS: Channel your magic to cast sunlight or summon a rain cloud above your plants. Evolve your plants from one stage to the next.

LOCAL MULTIPLAYER: Play in concert with your fellow sprout or aim for a higher individual score.

Context

Twin Sprouts showcases my ability to set and respect deadlines and address scope needs in order to deliver a finished product.

Twin Sprouts was developed by a team of 4 students at Cologne Game Lab as a semester project in a short period of 10 weeks. My tasks on this project were:

• Designing and documenting gameplay features,
• Creating and optimizing the game level in Unity to accomodate cooperative and competitive mindsets,
• Mocking up the user interface and defining its functionality,
• Organizing playtests and iterating accordingly on the game level and balance,
• Planning and monitoring the project, distributing tasks and handling presentations.

After its release, the game hit the front page of Itch.io and was covered by Rock, Paper, Shotgun and PC Gamer.
