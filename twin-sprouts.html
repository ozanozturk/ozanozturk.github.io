TWIN SPROUTS

ITCH.IO

Pick up seeds and grow plants in a vibrant environment!

Feed your plants with magic spells and gather the joy orbs they produce to increase your score.

Play cooperatively with or competitively against a friend.

Save your individual and combined team high scores on the leaderboard.

Twin Sprouts was developed by a team of 4 students at Cologne Game Lab as a semester project around the topics "finding joy" and "blossom" in a period of 2 months.

In addition to collaborating on gameplay design with other team members, producing documentation, organizing playtests and creating the level of the game, I took on the mantle of the project manager, managing the project pipeline around the main constraints of time and scope.

GENRE: Platformer

PLATFORM: PC, Linux

ENGINE: Unity

TEAM: 4

ROLE: Project Manager & Game Designer

DURATION: 2 months
