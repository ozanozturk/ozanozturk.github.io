SCHACHT

Schacht is a fast-paced 3D platformer focused on speedrunning and competition. Run on walls, swing, slide and dash to beat the levels as fast as possible. Compare your best times with your friends and with players from all around the world.

The game features four levels where you can learn the core mechanics of the game. You can then enjoy an infinite number of randomly generated levels put together from handcrafted segments.

You can also create your own levels using in the built-in level editor and challenge your friends.

﻿Schacht is now available on Steam.﻿

COMMENTS

Schacht was the first big group project I was a part of. We tried to emulate how game studios work by splitting tasks between departments of project management, game design, engineering, sound design and quality assurance. Agile development methodology was used with JIRA, and Git was our choice of version control for this project.

The one year I worked on Schacht was an excellent practice of functioning in a multidisciplinary and professional team as a game designer. Having worked on the game from pre-production to the release on Steam, I did not always see eye to eye with the lead designer about the direction of the game, but learning to work around these creative differences and focusing on bringing another designer's vision to reality without compromising it was an invaluable working experience to be had.
