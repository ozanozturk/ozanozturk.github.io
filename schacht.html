SCHACHT

STEAM PS STORE

Run, dash, jump! Schacht is an infinitely replayable 3D platformer featuring billions of procedurally-generated levels.

GENRE: 3D Platformer
PLATFORM: PC, PS4 
ENGINE: Unreal Engine 4 
TEAM: 35 
ROLE: Game Designer // Level Designer 
DURATION: 12 months, shipped

VERSATILE ABILITIES

Sprint on the ground, dash in the air, run along the walls, slide on slopes, swing with your grapple and hack deadly traps. Make your way to the end faster than anyone else can.

INFINITE REPLAYABILITY

Hone your skills in hand-built levels, or engage in randomized challenges to secure your place on the leaderboard. Schacht features over a billion level possibilities.

CHARACTER CUSTOMIZATION

Wings, skirts, mustache or baguettes... Dress your runner for any occasion.


Schacht is an example of my ability to collaborate with a vision holder and carry an idea from pitch to release in a sizeable team.

Schacht was greenlit by the Steam community and launched on PC in 2016. The game's PS4 release followed in 2018.

The game was exhibited at several events, including at the International Festival of Animated Film '17 and '18 in Stuttgart.

This project marked the first time I worked in a large team of game developers and collaborated with other game designers, as well as getting acquainted with industry standard tools such as Unreal Engine 4, Git, Confluence and JIRA. My tasks in this project were:

• Designing, documenting and refining character abilities including dash, grapple and pulse, 
• Collaborating with other game designers on the game design wiki on Confluence, 
• Representing the game design team at the weekly playtests organized by the QA team, 
• Evaluating user feedback and iterating accordingly on the tutorial system of the game in particular, 
• Creating the introductory tutorial level of the game in collaboration with the lead artist in Unreal Engine 4, 
• Designing, building and adjusting over two dozen level segments to seamlessly connect in procedurally generated levels and used in the in-game level editor.
