SCHACHT

STEAM
 PS STORE

Run, dash and jump! Race against time to complete challenging tracks!

Play alone or with up to 3 other players in procedurally generated levels.

Create and share your own levels with the in-game level editor.

Participate in daily, weekly and monthly challenges to rise to the top of the leaderboards.

Schacht is fast-paced 3D platformer developed by a diverse group of students at Stuttgart Media University. After a year of development, the game was greenlit by the Steam community and released for PC under the publishing label of the university in 2016. Two years after its debut on Steam, the game launched on PlayStation 4 and continued receiving updates thanks to the tireless efforts of my colleagues at Lab132.

Schacht was a game of many firsts for me: It was the first game I worked on from pre-production to release. It also marked the first time I worked in a large team of game developers and collaborated with other game designers, as well as getting acquainted with industry standard tools such as Unreal Engine 4, Confluence and JIRA.

GENRE: Platformer

PLATFORM: PC, PS4

ENGINE: Unreal Engine 4

TEAM: 35

ROLE: Game & Level Designer

DURATION: 12 months

▼ GAME DESIGN TASKS

- Pitched, designed, documented and refined numerous character abilities and level elements.

- Collaborated with other game designers to create and update the game design wiki on Confluence.

- Attended playtests and acted as a bridge between the game design and quality assurance teams.

- Adjusted my designs accordingly to the creative input from the lead designer.

▼ LEVEL DESIGN TASKS

- Created the introductory first level of the game in collaboration with the lead artist.

- Designed, built and adjusted different level segments to seamlessly connect in procedurally generated levels and the level editor.
