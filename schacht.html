SCHACHT

Schacht is a fast-paced 3D platformer focused on speedrunning and competition. Run on walls, swing, slide and dash to beat the levels as fast as possible. Compare your best times with your friends and with players from all around the world.

The game features four levels where you can learn the core mechanics of the game. You can then enjoy an infinite number of randomly generated levels put together from handcrafted segments.

You can also create your own levels using in the built-in level editor and challenge your friends.

﻿Schacht is now available on Steam.﻿
