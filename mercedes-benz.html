MERCEDES-BENZ
POCKET MUSEUM


Take a ride into the past of the automobile together with pioneering historic figures!

Augment your visit to the Mercedes-Benz Museum with on-location games.

Complete tasks in exhibits of the museum and build your own collection of cars.

Unlock various minigames to play after your visit and continue expanding your collection.

Mercedes-Benz Pocket Museum is a mobile museum game currently under development at Stuttgart Media University, slated for a 2018 release on mobile platforms.

The game is developed as a part of a joint research and development project between the Mercedes-Benz Museum and Stuttgart Media University.

I worked as the game designer of the project during and after my studies at Stuttgart Media University. The scope of my tasks extended into every corner of the design of the game, including mini-games, character dialogue, tasks and tutorial systems, unlocks and collections, and the user interface layout and structure.

GENRE: Collection

PLATFORM: iOS, Android

ENGINE: Unity

TEAM: +25

ROLE: Game Designer

DURATION: +9 months
