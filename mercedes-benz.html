MERCEDES-BENZ MUSEUM


The Mercedes-Benz Museum game is an on-location puzzle game enabling museum visitors to build their own collection of exhibited cars.

GENRE: Museum Game 
PLATFORM: Mobile 
ENGINE: Unity 
TEAM: +25 
ROLE: Game Designer 
DURATION: 8 months (my involvement), pending release

PreviousNextClose

DISCOVER

Look deeper into the dazzling exhibitions of the Mercedes-Benz Museum. Choose one of three pioneering figures from the Mercedes-Benz history to be your guide in the museum.

COLLECT

Complete a set of tasks with the help of your guide to collect your most desired car from each exhibition.

PLAY

Play fun minigames even after you have left the museum and continue expanding your collection with new cars and paints.

The Mercedes-Benz Museum game project shows how I can design a game for a very specific audience and setting in accordance with the needs of clients.

The game will be playable in the Mercedes-Benz Museum in Stuttgart soon.

Mercedes-Benz Pocket Museum is in development as part of research and development project between the Mercedes-Benz Museum and Stuttgart Media University. I worked as a game designer on the project during and after my studies at Stuttgart Media University.

The scope of my tasks extended into every corner of the design of the game, including:

• Co-designing the gameplay loop in a way to encourage exploration in the museum without the game taking the focus away from the exhibitions,
• Iterating on the game according to the feedback from the product owners,  
• Creating a set of museum tasks for each exhibition,  
• Pitching and designing three replayable minigames, each featuring three difficulty levels,  
• Writing all character dialogue for three historic guide characters in accordance with their documented personalities,
• Designing the tutorial system of the game,
• Mocking up the user interface and defining its flow and functionality,
• Pitching and designing the rewards, collections and unlocks systems.
