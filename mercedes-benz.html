MERCEDES-BENZ
POCKET MUSEUM


Take a ride into the past of the automobile with pioneering figures from history!

* Augment your visit to the Mercedes-Benz Museum with on-location games.

* Complete tasks in different exhibits and build your own collection of cars.

* Unlock various minigames to play after your visit and expand your collection.

PreviousNextClose

GENRE: Collection

PLATFORM: iOS, Android

ENGINE: Unity

TEAM: +25

ROLE: Game Designer

DURATION: 8 months

Description

Mercedes-Benz Pocket Museum is in development as part of research and development project between the Mercedes-Benz Museum and Stuttgart Media University.

The purpose of the game is not only to entertain the younger visitors of the museum, but also draw their attention to the finer details of the history and craftsmanship of Mercedes-Benz through playful minigames. 

Using the app in the museum, you can engage with historical figures such as Karl Benz, Wilhelm Maybach and Gottlieb Daimler to guide you in exhibitions and assist you in collecting unique cars for your collection.

The game is still in development at Stuttgart Media University and is expected to be playable locally at the Mercedes-Benz Museum in 2019.

Key Features

Discover: Look deeper into the dazzling exhibitions of the Mercedes-Benz Museum. Choose one of three pioneering figures from the Mercedes-Benz history to be your guide in the museum.

Collect: Complete a set of tasks with the help of your guide to collect your most desired car from each exhibition.

Play: Play one of four fun minigames even after you have left the museum and continue expanding your collection with new cars and paints.

My Contribution

I worked as a game designer on the project during and after my studies at Stuttgart Media University. The scope of my tasks extended into every corner of the design of the game, including:

* Designing the gameplay loop in a way to encourage exploration in the museum without the game taking the focus away from the exhibitions,
* Creating the set of museum tasks for earning cars from exhibitions,
* Designing three out of four replayable minigames, each featuring three difficulty levels,
* Writing all character dialogue for one of three histroic guide characters in accordance with their documented personalities,
* Creating mockups for user interface layout and functionality,
* Pitching and designing the collection and unlocks system.
