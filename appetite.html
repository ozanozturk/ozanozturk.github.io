APPETITE


GENRE: Digital Card Game

PLATFORM: Mobile

ENGINE: Unity

TEAM: 2

ROLE: Generalist Designer

DURATION: In development

PreviousNextClose

Create your own menu and master the calories!

• Your hand is your menu. Play food and drink cards from unique decks.
• Serve your insatiable customer with as much food and drinks as you can.
• Track calories in real time to keep your customer “fit" enough to keep paying.

Features

YOUR HAND, YOUR MENU: You are a restaurant manager. Create your own deck of food and drinks. Purchase new cards with in-game currency and expand your collection.

SERVED AS ASKED: Watch out for the ever-changing wants of your customer to whet his appetite. Unlock and use special gourmet cards on dishes to bolster their taste and price.

CARBS OR NO CARBS: Pay attention to the energy values of the food and drinks you serve to ensure your customer remains "fit" enough to keep paying.

Context

Appetite is an example of how I take ordinary inspirations from daily life and morph them into well-thought-out game concepts without losing their connection to reality.

The work on Appetite started at the Ubisoft Blue Byte Game Jam when I pitched it on the topic of "health". In addition to being an addictively entertaining game, Appetite is also designed to raise awareness about healthy eating through an understanding of how energy values of food and drinks work. 

I have recently picked the project up once again to develop it further in my spare time with a colleague. In this project, I act as a generalist designer responsible for all aspects of the project, save for programming. Among my tasks are:

• Designing and documenting all gameplay features,
• Creating the user interface,
• Balancing the in-game economy based on realistic energy values of food and drink cards.
