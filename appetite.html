APPETITE


Create your own menu and master the calories!

* Your hand is your menu. Draw and play a card from unique decks.

* Serve your insatiable customer with as much food and drinks as you can.

* Track calories in real time to keep your customer “healthy" enough to keep paying.

GENRE: Digital Card Game

PLATFORM: Mobile

ENGINE: Unity

TEAM: 2

ROLE: Designer

DURATION: Ongoing

Description

Appetite is a card-based, real-time strategy game for mobile platforms. As a restaurant manager, you are tasked with serving your customer as much food and drinks as possible–especially expensive ones.

As your guest will eat and drink almost anything you serve them without any concern for their own health or size, it is your responsibility to keep him alive–and paying.

Beyond its entertainment value, Appetite aims to raise awareness about about the energy values of food and promote healthier eating habits through irony.

The work on Appetite started at the Ubisoft Blue Byte Game Jam when I pitched it on the topic of "health". I have recently picked the project up once again to develop it further in my spare time with a colleague.

Key Features

YOUR HAND, YOUR MENU: Build a custom deck of cards in four categories—Main Dish, Vegetarian, Dessert and Drinks. Purchase new cards with in-game currency and expand your collection.

SERVED AS ASKED: Watch out in real-time for the ever-changing wants of your customer. Serve according to his preferences to whet his appetite while making sure he's fit enough to keep eating.

GOURMET EXPERIENCE: Need some sauce for your pasta? Unlock special gourmet cards and use them on compatible food or drinks to bolster their taste and price.

My Contribution

In this project, I act as a generalist designer responsible for all aspects of the project, save for programming. Among my tasks are:

* Designing and documenting all gameplay features,
* Creating the user interface,
* Balancing different decks of food and drink cards using real energy values.
