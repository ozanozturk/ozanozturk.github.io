APPETITE


Appetite is a strategy card game where you must serve your insatiable customer with as much food as possible while ensuring he's "fit" enough to keep paying.

GENRE: Digital Card Game
PLATFORM: Android 
ENGINE: Unity 
TEAM: 2 
ROLE: Generalist Designer 
DURATION: +3 months, in development

YOUR HAND, YOUR MENU

You are a restaurant manager. Create your own deck of food and drinks. Purchase new cards with in-game currency and expand your collection.

SERVED AS ASKED

Watch out for the ever-changing wants of your customer. Unlock and use special gourmet cards on dishes to bolster their taste and price.

CARBS OR NO CARBS

Pay attention to the energy values of the food and drinks you serve to ensure your customer remains "fit" enough to keep paying.

Appetite is an example of how I take ordinary inspirations from daily life and morph them into well-thought-out game concepts without losing their connection to reality.

In addition to being an addictively entertaining game, Appetite is also designed to raise awareness about healthy eating through an understanding of how energy values of food and drinks work. 

The work on Appetite started at the Ubisoft Blue Byte Game Jam when I pitched it on the topic of "health".

I have recently picked the project up once again to develop it further in my spare time with a colleague. In this project, I act as a generalist designer responsible for all aspects of the project, save for programming. Among my tasks are:

• Designing and documenting all gameplay features, 
• Creating the user interface, 
• Balancing the in-game economy based on realistic energy values of food and drink cards.
