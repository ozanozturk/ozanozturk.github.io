APPETITE


Create your own menu and master the calories!

* Your hand is your menu. Draw and play a card from unique decks.

* Serve your insatiable customer with as much food and drinks as you can.

* Track calories in real time and keep your customer “healthy" enough to keep paying.

Appetite is a card-based, real-time strategy game where players take on the role of a restaurant manager with the simple goal of serving your customer as much food and drinks as possible–especially expensive ones.

As your guest will eat and drink almost anything you serve them without any concern for their own health or size, it is your responsibility to keep him alive–and paying.

Beyond being an addictively-entertaining digital card game, Appetite aims to raise awareness about about the calorie values of food and promote more careful, healthier eating.

Appetite is currently in development for mobile platforms.

GENRE: Card

PLATFORM: Mobile

ENGINE: Unity

TEAM: 2

ROLE: Designer

DURATION: Ongoing
